Description

The wardens are the caretakers of the oubliette. Once benign guardians of the dominions, they are bound to the lands and creatures under their care. As their realms have withered and twisted under blight and darkness, so have they. Their hatred and despair have festered for millennia, giving rise at length to madness and obsession. For the immeasurable suffering and loss that has befallen them, many blame mankind as the source of the blight. This hatred is compounded with envy for the love of the sovereigns, which was always denied to them. In the misery of mankind, they seek vengeance against the memory of their former overlords. Abandoned and betrayed, desperate to preserve their fading lands against all odds and at any price, the wardens had recourse to dark and terrible actions. But these days they need little excuse
