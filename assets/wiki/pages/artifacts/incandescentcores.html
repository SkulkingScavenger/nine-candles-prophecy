The light that exists within the forsaken is pale and dim, a reflection of a fire has been long lost, as the moon reflects the sun.

However, there once dwelt in the oubliette creatures of True Flame. Their incandescence remains within their smoldering cores, coveted by wardens and gremlins alike.
Incandescent cores are potently, dangerously magical. Their uses are many, their power virtually inexhaustible.

For Gremlins, they mean something still more: a righting of old wrongs, a return to a past glory, to a time when they rode the high winds on sable wings
