Oubliette horses are quick and powerful, with translucent, hairless skin and eyeless skulls. They have human hands and long, black tongues that whisper to the air in a forgotten language. Unlike surface horses, they are agile in the caves, able to wriggle rapidly through tight spaces and climb over ledges.

If one wishes to be accepted as a rider by one, they must first whisper their darkest secret into it's ear, but be careful: they always know when they are lied to.
