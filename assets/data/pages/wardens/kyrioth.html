Lord Kyrioth does not ride the black wind, but crawls among the corrupted soil in the forests north of Grayhaven, in what were once the vast churchyards of Sherab. at other times he walks among humankind, possessing both the dead and those of the living who are bound to him: his masks

#INTRO_END
With the passing of the cycles of feast and desolation, Kyrioth has grown malcontent. Though a powerful necromancer, he is least among the seven who stand before Zahastaroth's throne, and his influence weakens each time the city rises and falls. mutual distrust and hatred of his rivals combined with disgust for his near-mindless overlord has led him to seek out holdings of his own and to scheme against an inevitable betrayal
