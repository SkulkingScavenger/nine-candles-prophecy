Shaman to the Magakash Clan, Gray-eyes is known best for his role as adviser to Warlord Throatbiter, bitter disagreements with the rest of the Gremlin Shamans, and fabled powers of prophecy.

While his ability to predict the future is a matter of polarizing opinion, his influence over gremlin society is beyond dispute (Whether that's a good thing is another story).
The reigning warlord, at least, seems to hang upon the shaman's words, apparently unconcerned with his advisor's reputation for duplicity.
