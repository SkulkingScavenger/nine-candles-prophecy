Prince of the Shamblers, the necromancer of Hekkath, father of ghouls...

The wizard Akat was instrumental in the fall of Hekkath, first convincing his nephew to betray the Seshek, then betraying his nephew in turn in order to secure a pact with Lord Shathoth.
In the end, Akat got what he wanted: the secret to immortality and the halting of the blight. Now he rules alone upon his rotting throne, over a kingdom of the damned

Today, Akat leads the dreg industry and manages the reaping of souls from his Tomb Palace in the crypts.
