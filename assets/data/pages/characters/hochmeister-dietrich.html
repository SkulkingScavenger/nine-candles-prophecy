Dietrich "The True" was elected Grand Master of the Silver Fountain at the age of 42 after his predecessor succumbed to the blight.

His reign has been steady. Stagnant, some might say, though never to his face.
In any event, he has seemed to show greater interest in renovating Grayhaven Cathedrals than expanding the boundaries of the beacon-lights.

Despite this, he was praised even by his critics for his valiant and timely action during the Gremlin Wars, for which he is credited as possibly saving the city itself from devastation.
