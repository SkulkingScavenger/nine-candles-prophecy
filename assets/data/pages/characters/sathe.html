In the oubliette, the dead must be treated with special care to ensure the angry spirit does not return as a shade or ghoul to plague its living relatives. The art of shackling souls to charnel flesh and anchoring them to a grave is an esoteric art, with secret funerary techniques passed down from one mortician to another through long generations.
Although their work is dark and strange and not so very different from witchcraft, their vital importance within the villages makes them the object of both fear and reverence.

In Grayhaven and New Tharas, morticians operate under the auspices of the undertakers guild, a secretive and powerful organization whose Grand Morticians wield great influence, surpassing even members of the nobility in some cases.

Such a creature is the man known as Sathe, a hard-dealing, foul-tempered magician with a great many shadowy dealings and powerful contacts in New Tharas. If you're looking for a body, or need to make one disappear, he can hook you up, but you had better be able to pay...
