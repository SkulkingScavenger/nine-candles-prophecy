Younger brother to the Baron of Pestmarch, Sir Athelstein is not a likeable man.
Bitter, reclusive, and given over to melancholy, he lives alone in his rotting tower without family or servants.

After a lifetime at the edge of the darklands, the blight has taken its toll, though for a time the Silver Laudanum kept it at bay. Now the last of his wealth is spent and his supply has dwindled at last to a single draught

He sits brooding in his chair, disdaining the comfort of a bed, waiting for the end to come
