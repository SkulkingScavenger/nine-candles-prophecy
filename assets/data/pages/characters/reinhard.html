A dispossesed knight of Grayhaven. Reinhard's father was Baron of Finsterwald before he was stripped of his lands and titles by the machinations of a man he had thought his friend.

Without hope of inheritance, and bitter at his former overlord, Reinhard went north to serve as a lay mercenary for the Order of the Silver Fountain.

Now, after five years of hard campaigning against the gremlins, he has returned to Grayhaven as a vagabond knight to seek revenge against Baron Isengrim, and the restoration of his ancestral lands
