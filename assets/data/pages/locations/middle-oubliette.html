As one descends deeper, below the old ruins, the caverns become more dangerous.
If nothing else there are fewer humans, and those who do live here are seen as desperate and more likely to be hostile.

While resources are actually more plentiful, speaking objectively, they are often not as readily consumed. Water must be more thoroughly sanitized, food must have poisons carefully removed. the paths less traveled and rest-stops further between. Weather is worse.

And of course, there are more monsters and worse monsters
