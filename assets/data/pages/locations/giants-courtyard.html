A place of eerie silence, and vast stone columns carved by an unknown hand. An air of ancient power hangs over it, demanding reverence

The Giants' Courtyard is a vastcavern near the top of the oubliette, far to the north of Sherab. It's true name has been largely forgotten, but the appelation of "Giants' Court" refers to the tremendous monuments, columns and arches that seem far too large for any human force to have constructed them. At the northern edge of the cavern sits the ruins of a vast temple, its great stairs shattered.
