It is said that in ancient times the ivory spires of Varasa, the most sacred and glorious of all Tharas' cathedrals, rose above the clouds, gleaming like starlight. It was within that highest tower of that the Tharas priest-kings congregated to hold their grand synod.
And it was here that the high priests sought refuge when their city sank beneath the waves, and it was here that they were devoured for their sins.

Sunken deep below the slime and blackened by years of accumulated filth, the once gleaming spires of Varasa serve as the lair of Tharas' depraved archwarden.
