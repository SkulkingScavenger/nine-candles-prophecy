It is said that, in ancient times, the ivory spires of Varasa rose above the clouds, gleaming like starlight: the most sacred and glorious of all Tharas' cathedrals.
There, within its highest tower, the Tharas priest-kings would congregate to hold their grand synod.
It was there that the high priests sought refuge when their city sank beneath the waves, and it was there that at last they were devoured for their sins.

Sunken deep below the slime and blackened by millenia of accumulated filth, the once gleaming spires of Varasa now serve as the lair of Tharas' depraved archwarden.
