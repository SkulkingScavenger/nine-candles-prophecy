There is a chamber in a cathedral in the depths of Ahoth, whose doors have been sealed since before the dawn of the crimson sun.

In that circle nine thrones stand in a circle. Three lie broken and shattered on the floor

An old crone, older than ahoth itself, tends to a white fire in a brazier at the center of the room.

One day the lords will return to their thrones at the end of all cycles, and until then the fire must be kept alight

For the sake of the prophecy which was spoken from the black mirror on the day the Stairs were shattered...

For the prophecy of nine candles
