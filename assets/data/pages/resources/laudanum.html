Liquid atonement, the tears of the Sorrow Worms who eternally mourn for the fate of the oubliette and those trapped within it. It is a potent substance of safety and healing, one of the only reliable remedies for the blight. Certainly the most readily available.

It is not without it's own dangers, however. The respite from sorrow and fear can be addictive, and in large quantities it causes forgetfulness as memories corrode. It is a poison to demons and other creatures of nightmare, who are themselves little more than memories, and thus an important component in warding circles.
