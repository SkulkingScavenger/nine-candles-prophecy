Most commonly the work of passing Seshek, but perhaps also a dormant candle-tree forest or the lair of an eccentric hermit. Or... well I won't go into it.
While it is perhaps suspsicious for an uninhabited cave hundreds of miles away from civilization to be so generously illuminated, in the Oubliette things like this are positively mundane

Regardless, such caves make for a welcome respite for weary travelers, and a source of coveted seshek candles for the very lucky. Better not to take too many though: it is said to be very bad luck to get greedy with gravekeeper candles. But then again, it is also bad luck to run out of candles...
