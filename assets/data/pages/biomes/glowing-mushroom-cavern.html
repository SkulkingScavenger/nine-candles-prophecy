The soft light of a glowing cavern is a welcome sight to any weary traveler

The fabled Forest of Lights is an obvious example, but most are not so ostentatious.
The most common species of glowing mushroom gives off only a simple blue-white light.

These caves are much rarer than ordinary mushroom forests (perhaps 15% of mushroom forests are luminous) and generally do not overlap, as the giant mushrooms tend to squeeze out their smaller, more delicate cousins.
