AS IT WAS IN THE BEGINNING SO SHALL IT BE IN THE END

When hope and fear have passed away, a memory will remain
When the last memory has been forgotten, there will be a true ending

What was white shall run red
What had been called precious will be abandoned
What was sought will be found
And the finding of it will be a greater tragedy than the loss

When the last betrayal has been revealed then will look to the vaults
Those who worshipped the flesh that rots will mourn it's loss And they will count the bones of the dead as their inheritance

When the Nine Candles have been lit the forsaken will walk upon the shattered stairs
In their blindness they will stumble and curse the light that looks not on the path
And those who reach the heights will weep at their misfortune
