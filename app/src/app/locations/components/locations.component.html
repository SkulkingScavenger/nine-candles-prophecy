The Oubliette

It is a vast labyrinth of twisting caverns and dismal dungeons
It is a place of long and lonely darkness,
a place to wander forever in solitude and silence
It is a place of terror and a place of wonder
Perhaps, one day, you may even find what you have come here to seek
