Oubliette Terrain

For those who wander long within the labyrinthine ever-shifting depths of the oubliette: navigators, candleseekers and exiles of all kinds... every passage and corridor holds a vital significance. Any resource, or lack thereof, may be the difference between life and death (such as it is)

There are, of course, the well-known landmarks, but there is often a need to speak generally of certain recurring features. This is particularly the case in regions where the oubliette is more temperamental and changes more quickly.
