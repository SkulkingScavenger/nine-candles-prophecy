Gravekeeper Quest

The Gravekeeper has a quest for you!

Important Features
  • 10 players max (make your friends buy the game if its too hard for you)
  • dress up your person as a knight or peasant (your choice)
  • dark side and lightside options (lightside is good, darkside is evil)
  • starving
  • hallucinations
  • crafting
  • houses
  • horses
  • ghosts
  • goblins
  • better than valheim (eventually)


Backstory

Basically, there's this guy. He's the last gravekeeper (gravekeepers are the ones who help you when you're dead. they're good guys).

You and your friends are dead, and this guy brings you back to life to find out what happened to his friends and fix their graveyards so the ghosts stop

Then basically, your job is to figure out the mystery. but if you don't want to do that you can screw the guy over and just build a house or something and enjoy being a skeleton who rides horses. This game is open world like that. You don't have to do things just because the NPC tells you to (its a free country. do whatever).

How to win

  • WASD to move, mouse to fight, space to jump.
  • Cut trees for wood
  • Break rocks for rocks
  • Kill animals for meat
  • Kill badguys or else
  • Hide in your house when you're scared
  • Fight every boss
  • Watch the end credits
  • Tell your friends to buy it
It's that easy

Hints

  • To make the game harder and show how tough you are, never save. Delete your save file if you save accidentally. don't dodge any of the bosses attacks, just tank it like a man
  • Turn on friendly fire if your friends make you mad
