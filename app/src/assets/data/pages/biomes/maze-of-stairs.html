As the reality of the oubliette corrodes under the creeping putrefaction, vast chasms have begun to appear.
Not all are empty, or even unpassable: the maze of stairs meanders throughout the oubliette, connecting the dominions with it's ever-shifting web.

navigating the stairs is exceedingly difficult and dangerous. Individual patches of stairs are not guaranteed to link up to each other... or to anything. Even the most veteran navigators might well hesitate to trust them for long journeys
