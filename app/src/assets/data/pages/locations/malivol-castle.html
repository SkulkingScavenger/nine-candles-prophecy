The accursed citadel of Lord Grimnas, archwarden of Korhazar, wherein he holds his nightmarish court.

Malivol castle rises like a mountain at the heart of a dimly burning cavern below the city of Korhazar. The air here is stifling hot, the sky filled with dark winged creatures. In the distance the wailing of a thousand tormented shades echo from the depths

upon the soaring and twisted ramparts of screaming stones prowl hideous devils, and the bridge is kept by the great demon Morvias

Despite its terrible aspect, the castle is always open to those who seek an audience with lord grimnas. By order of the crimson tyrant, such guests are not to be hindered, and those who come freely may leave as they came. And he is a most hospitable host: The grand feasts of Malivol far excel any mortal fare, and the eternal night is passed in all manner of unearthly entertainments.
And yet for all that, it is really quite a foolish thing to visit the court of such a creature...
