One of the oldest monsters of the Oubliette is a great tree. Once, long ago, an ancient forest was brought down into the oubliette, and without the sun, many of the trees began to die. However, that great black oak was too old and powerful to die. It's roots twisted and delved deep, into hekkath and beyond, growing as dark and tangled as the darkroot with which in time they mingled

Thus was born the Labyrinth of Roots, a vast catacomb of corrupted, living wood.
