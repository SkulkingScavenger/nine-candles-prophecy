Irongate Castle is the ancestral seat of the haunted von Schwarzschild family and administrative center of the eponymous Grayhaven barony.

The castle lies on the far northern border of Grayhaven Territory, well out of sight of the city's beacons and butting up against a vast wilderness of darkroot and mouldering ruins. Like most such darkland castles, it is well fortified and equipped with towering beacons that illuminate the surrounding farmland and provide a sense of time and direction to the vassals and serfs who are bound to it.
