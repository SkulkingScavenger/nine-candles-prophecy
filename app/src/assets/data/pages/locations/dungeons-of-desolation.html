A vast, meandering labyrinth. It's winding, reinforced vaults and dismal cells resemble a prison, but who would need a prison the size of a city? In any event, these days it is home mainly to ghosts and monsters

That having been said, a few crazed spirits seem intent on keeping the place operational in spite of its dilapidation and their own deaths.
Thanks to such efforts, it is not uncommon to end an expedition at the bottom of a half-flooded cell with no food or water, surrounded by the gnawed bones of those who likely starved to death before you.

Now, such old dungeons are not particularly uncommon in the Oubliette, but there is a peculiar enchantment over these: A dark power slumbers in the depths, bloodthirsty and vengeful.
It drags down the guilty like an anchor to their destruction, to join the legions of crazed shades who wander its corridors
