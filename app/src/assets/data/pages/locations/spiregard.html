An old castle in the bogs near Hearthfire, renovated into a fortified monastery by the Order of the Silver Fountain to serve as an outpost from which to check gremlin raids on the grayhaven-hearthfire trade route.

There has been no news from Spiregard Abbey in many years however. Emissaries sent to investigate the silence have never returned. The gremlin attacks from the western clans have yet to resume, but there are rumors that some darker fate has befallen the knight-brothers...
