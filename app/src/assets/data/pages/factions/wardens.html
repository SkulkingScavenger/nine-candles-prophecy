#EPIGRAPH_END
The wardens are sinister entities who rule over the dark places of the oubliette: ancient guardians of the dominions, bound to the lands and creatures under their care.
As their realms have withered and twisted under blight and darkness, so have they.

They lurk in dark corners, in deep places and in the black wind, and are always waiting deep beneath the veil of sleep, in the horrible crimson realm of nightmare and fever-dream where the walls of reality have been peeled away

It is necessary to appease them where they cannot be avoided, but it is always dangerous to become too familiar. Though in times past they may once have shown benevolence to those who gave proper respect, their hatred and despair have festered for millennia, giving rise at length to madness and obsession.

The lesser ones are capricious creatures, sometimes giving aid, sometimes working mischief, although as the dominions fade they have grown darker and more sinister
The greater ones are terrible beings, fearful to treat with yet still willing in some cases to give aid to mortals... for a price of course. Others, given over to hatred or hunger prowl the darkness as terrible forces of destruction and subversion, and woe to those who meet them on the road

In all cases, their concern for the feeding of the crimson sun has made them bloodthirsty. Fearful sacrifices have been made, and yet still more must be offered in the days to come

Hierarchy of Ascendance

Creatures of ancient oaths and terrible duties, warden society in turn forms an invisible hierarchy.

Conceptualized (Demiwarden)
Incomplete wardens, not yet wholly distinct from the consciousness of their progenitor, charged with minor tasks unworthy of their superior's full attention. Often tasked with overseeing mortals under a greater warden's authority
Summoning Sigils: 3

Consecrated (Infrawarden, Lesser Warden)
Emissaries, spies, and attendants to more powerful wardens.
Summoning Sigils: 5

Venerated (Mesowarden)
The rank and file of warden society: agents, guardians and reapers fulfilling their duties independantly
Summoning Sigils: 7

Exalted (Hierowarden, Greater Warden)
The powers that govern the oubliette and supervise the lesser wardens
Summoning Sigils: 9

Paragon (Archwarden)
The Archwarden caste reigns supreme within the oubliette, each a god of its own dominion, charged with preserving the memory of the realms
Summoning Sigils: 13+

Animating Principles

Form is shifting, subject to errors in perception and disemulation. The unseen truth is fixed, bound by burning oaths and secret names. At the core of every warden is an animating principle.
Though each warden has unique duties and powers, this core serves as a rough form of classification

Volition
The force that manifests, by which thoughts are made real

Conviction
The discernment of truth, the judgement of sins

Ideation
The conception of possibilities

Perception
The illusions of external reality

Preservation
The persistence of memory that transcends temporality

Cognition
The awareness of internal reality, the cohesion of ideas

Adhesion
The formation of chains, of bonds and of anchors

Coordination
The exertion of authority, the formation of hierarchy

Transmutation
The process of becoming what is not and the abandonment of what has been
