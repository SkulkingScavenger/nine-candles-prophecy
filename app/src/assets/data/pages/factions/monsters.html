When the dominions first succumbed to the putrefaction, a wave of extinctions followed as forests withered and oceans dried and all life was given over to desolation.
Those few who survived in the darkness that followed were changed. countless cycles spent in darkness, feeding on death and upon the uncanny darkroot, has left their mark forever.

Now they and their spawn fill the oubliette. Many are the last of their kind, or nearly so. The wardens did what they could, and in many cases, even in their madness they still hold the loyalty of the creatures under their care, who they saved from annihilation

However, many beasts still crave the true light whose essence is reflected in the blood of the forsaken. Many are drawn to the villages and outposts, hunting for the flesh of man wherever it may be found
