Marlok's first memory was waking in a deep vault in the Dungeons of Desolation along with three mysterious oblong boxes

In his efforts to escape the dungeons and recover his memory, Marlok made the acquaintance of the scholar Alfred Fletcher, whose research into the nature of the shademark and the forces at work in the Oubliette suggested that he might find the key to his lost memories within the lost dominion of Sinra.

After escaping the dungeons via the dark bridge and the maze of stairs, Marlok found his progress barred by A great passage, where he was informed by Krikirrik that the doorway to the dominion would be opened only when the Nine Candles had been lit, but that the truth of the Shademark indeed lay beyond.
