The black liquescent god of the catacombs, Lord Shathoth is a creature of living madness, horrifically transformed by the blight he sought to master. He ceaselessly patrols the depths of his horrible lair in the crypts of Hekkath, devouring the rotting souls entombed therein.

The ancient Hekkathan god of death, Lord Shathoth has pretensions to rule supreme among the archwardens in the absence of Mathranoath, yet is willing to bide his time. He has faced fierce opposition from Lord Grimnas and Lord Shiragazira, but time is in many ways on his side.
