Do not be abroad when the sour winds blow, when the black rains fall and the jakilaunts call.
For on nights such as these, Lord Sarogoth rides forth upon the sable wind, seeking for the lost and the weary. Woe to those who meet him upon the road, for those who hear his voice and approach the light of his lantern are never seen again... With some notable exceptions.
#INTRO_END
Lord Sarogoth was once a benevolent spirit, and though these days he has gone quite mad, he retains his unearthly charisma, and the power of his voice to command the living and the dead has not diminished
