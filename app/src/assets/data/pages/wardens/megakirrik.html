there's nothing you can do now
to damage or to mend
Although it's not completed,
your journey's at an end

So rest, my old companion,
lay down your heavy load
And close the eyes so weary
that glimpsed me on the road

For though you entertained me,
My most elusive prey,
it never was in question
which of us would win the day

Our time is growing short now
I feel you going cold
A tender last goodbye for
my fiery foe of old

But I have just one question
for you, here at the last
Some things are only clear when
all else is in the past

Does anything have meaning
Now all's about to fade?
those high ideals and noble dreams
still sparkle in the shade?

No friends are coming with you
forgotten and alone
for debts unpaid and deeds undone
you never shall atone

The righteous works you strived for
How many came to pass?
The tiny rays of sunshine...
to what do they amass?

You tried to make a difference
and briefly you glowed bright:
a fleeting spark of fire
in an everlasting night

the principles that led you
were planted in your head
by fools who came before you
and whither have they led?

Your kindness, oft misguided,
brought misery and pain
how much was self-deception?
how much was done in vain?

Do little sins disturb you?
Are you worse than you believed?
or does everything seem pointless
and now you're just relieved?

You used to be so violent
so loud and self-assured
But now you've gone all quiet
You've hardly said a word

Perhaps you've come to realize
the folly of your plan

For this world belongs to monsters...
...And you were just a man
