Holding court within his accursed castle, The archwarden of Korhazar rules over his rotting dominion in dark splendor.

One of the more approachable of the archwardens, the gates of Malivol are always open to supplicants. One must be truly desperate to seek a boon of the Tyrant Sorcerer however. To stand in the presence of such a power, even briefly, seldom leaves one unchanged, and those who accept his blessings often learn too late what a terrible price they have paid.
